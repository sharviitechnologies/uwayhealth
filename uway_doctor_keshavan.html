About Dr. Keshavan

Dr. P.T. Keshavan Nambisan represents the 5th generation of an illustrious family of traditional Vaidyas from the Old Malabar region of Kerala. His lineage carries forward a tradition of classical Ayurvedic practice that predates formal medical institutions — and he combines that ancestral depth with rigorous modern clinical training.

Before co-founding UWAY, Dr. Keshavan served as Deputy Chief Medical Officer at The Arya Vaidya Chikitsalayam (AVP) in Coimbatore — one of the most respected Ayurveda institutions in India. His 24+ years of practice spans patients from more than 70 countries, primarily those managing complex, progressive, and autoimmune conditions for which conventional medicine offers only management, not resolution.

He is not a generalist. His practice is deliberately focused on conditions that are chronic, complex, and underserved by standard treatment pathways — which is precisely the patient UWAY was built to serve.

Research & institutional credentials

Active AYUSH Government Research — Co-PI
AMRA Study: Efficacy of Classical Ayurveda Management vs Methotrexate in Rheumatoid Arthritis
This Ministry of AYUSH-funded randomised clinical study directly compares classical Ayurvedic treatment protocols against Methotrexate — the gold-standard DMARD for RA — in patients with confirmed disease. Dr. Keshavan serves as Co-Principal Investigator, bringing his 24+ years of RA clinical experience to a formally structured, government-backed evidence framework.
Funded by Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India · Active study, ongoing enrolment

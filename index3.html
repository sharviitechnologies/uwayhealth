Understanding rheumatoid arthritis

Rheumatoid arthritis is a chronic autoimmune condition where the immune system mistakenly attacks the joints — causing inflammation, pain, swelling, and over time, joint damage. Unlike osteoarthritis, which is mechanical wear, RA is driven by immune dysfunction that can affect the whole body.

Most patients who come to UWAY for RA have already been on DMARDs or steroids — often experiencing relief that plateaus, or side effects that make long-term use difficult. Ayurveda addresses RA differently: by working on the immune dysregulation (termed Ama accumulation in classical texts) rather than only suppressing the inflammatory response.

Frequently asked questions

Can Ayurveda cure rheumatoid arthritis permanently? +
RA is a chronic autoimmune condition — no system of medicine offers a guaranteed permanent cure. What Ayurveda can do is significantly reduce inflammation, improve joint function, and in many cases lower or eliminate dependency on DMARDs. Our doctors will give you an honest, case-specific assessment in your first consultation.
Can I take Ayurveda medicines alongside Methotrexate? +
Yes, in most cases. UWAY doctors are experienced with integrative management. Your doctor will review your current medications and advise on what can be continued alongside Ayurvedic treatment.
How is RA diagnosed and monitored at UWAY? +
Your doctor will review existing blood reports (RF, Anti-CCP, CRP, ESR) and imaging. Follow-up tests are advised at regular intervals — Ayurveda at UWAY uses modern diagnostics to measure outcomes, not just symptom reports.
